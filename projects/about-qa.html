About Q.A

This is a game within a game! :) You are playing as a confident game developer who is struggling to fix the bugs for the game he is working on the night before release. Play through the different phases of bug fixing including Denial, Frustration, Beg for Mercy and OS update and try to play the level you have made once without any bugs. You can swith between the game window and debug window to find and fix bugs. Good Luck!

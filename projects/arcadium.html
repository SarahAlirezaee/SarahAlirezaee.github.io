Arcadium

Arcadium is freemium PvP mobile game in which players compete within different leaderboards by betting against one-another and challenging each-other playing mini-games that put their soft mental skills like simple math, reaction timing, peripheral reading , accuracy and etc to a test. Plyers can farm gold and XP by winning challenges, accomplishing seasonal missions and participating in tournaments and grind their way into the top of different leaderboards.
